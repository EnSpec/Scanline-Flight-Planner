Drawning Help

Adding a Scan Area:

After clicking , click on the map to place the vertices of your desired scan area.

Modifying Scan Areas:

Click on the the starting vertex again to complete the scan area. You can click and drag a completed area to move it or right click it to delete it. Double click on the map to begin placing another scan area.

Completing Scan Areas:

Once you have added all desired scan areas, click . A flight path will automatically be generated through the scan areas. The route can be adjusted via the control panel at the bottom of the page.
